## Airtable Community 文案

**标题**: DataBridge — AI cleaning & translation tool for your Airtable bases

**正文**:
Hi Airtable community! I built DataBridge to solve a common pain point: dirty data and language barriers in Airtable.

What it does:
- Connects to your Airtable base via PAT (read + write)
- AI-powered cleaning: trim, dedup, standardize, fix emails/phones
- Translation: EN↔ZH↔JA and more via DeepSeek
- Pay-per-use pricing, no subscriptions
- Results write back to your Airtable (new column or overwrite)

Try it free: https://databridge.app (5 free translations + 5 free cleanings)

Built with Node.js + Express. Would love your feedback!
