① Click here to create a PAT on Airtable ↗
② Copy-paste it below. We only need read & write data permission.

1. Open Airtable token page ↗ (new tab)
2. Click "Create new token", name it "TableTurn"
3. Check these boxes:
　☑ data.records:read
　☑ data.records:write
　☑ schema.bases:read
4. Copy the token (starts with pat...) and paste it above
5. Find your Base ID from the URL:
　airtable.com/appXXXXXXX/tableName

🔒 Your PAT is stored only in your browser and never sent to our server.

Connect your Notion database with an Internal Integration Token

Create a Notion integration at notion.so/my-integrations ↗

1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations ↗
2. Click "New integration", name it "TableTurn"
3. Select your workspace, click Submit
4. Copy the "Internal Integration Secret" (starts with ntn_)
5. In Notion, open your database → ... menu → Connections → Add TableTurn
6. Paste the token above and click Connect

🔒 Your token stays in your browser, never stored on our server.
